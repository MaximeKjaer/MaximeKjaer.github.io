About me

Hello! My name is Maxime Kjaer, and I'm an 18-year-old Computer Science student. Originally from Copenhagen, Denmark, I'm now living and studying at the EPFL, the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne.

When I'm not studying or programming, I like running, wasting my time on Netflix or talking to my friends.

Introductions aside, this site won't be a shrine of self-celebration (I can't stand that). No, I'll mainly use it as an outlet for my thoughts, ideas and work, should anyone want to read into that.
